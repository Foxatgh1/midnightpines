Press Start to enter the woods.
How to play: Move with WASD or Arrow keys. You are the yellow survivor. The camera is zoomed in, so you must explore the forest map: navigate around tree obstacles and avoid all creatures while collecting the 4 glowing relics. Once all relics are collected, your state changes to Hunt: touch creatures to eliminate them. Eliminate all 4 to win. If any creature touches you before all relics are collected, you lose.

Creature AI: 1) The Wanderer moves randomly and is the fastest. 2) The Blinker always chases you, is slowest, and teleports close if very far away. 3) The Stalker directly targets you and is second slowest. 4) The Shifter alternates between chasing and random movement.
