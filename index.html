Press Start to enter the woods.
How to play: Move with WASD or Arrow keys. You are the yellow survivor. The camera is zoomed in, so you must explore the forest map: navigate around tree obstacles and avoid all creatures while collecting the 4 glowing relics. Relic #1 effect: grants 1 extra life. Relic #2 effect: increases your movement speed by 10%. Relic #3 effect: shows arrows that point to the other relics. Relic #4 effect: none yet. Hidden passage: find it and press Space while standing over it to enter a basement. Touch basement stairs to return to the woods. Time is paused while underground. Cellar relic effect: teleports you to center, removes the first 4 enemies, and starts a fleeing Eater hunt. After catching that Eater, you become the Eater in an endless mode where fleeing yellow survivors keep respawning. Win condition: collect all 4 relics, then eliminate the first 4 enemies. If any creature touches you before that, you lose.

Creature AI: 1) The Wanderer moves randomly and is the fastest. 2) The Blinker always chases you, is slowest, and teleports close if very far away. 3) The Stalker directly targets you and is second slowest. 4) The Shifter alternates between chasing and random movement. 5) The Eater spawns at 1:00, phases through trees, and is invincible.
